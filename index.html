PLArmory

At PLArmory, we combine the timeless craftsmanship of wood with the modern precision of 3D printing. Each sword starts with a solid wooden core, then receives a custom 3D-printed overlay to bring your fantasy weapon to life. Whether you’re a cosplayer, LARPer, or collector, our props are designed to impress and endure. Please note that our props are NOT meant to be used in any combat scenarios and are NOT rated for any LARP scenarios, and PLArmory or any of its associated parties are not responsible for damages or injuries sustained due to improper use of our props.
